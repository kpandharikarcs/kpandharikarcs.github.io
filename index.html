The Integration Wizard

Every enterprise integration starts the same way: weeks of discovery, brittle point-to-point connections, and data that never quite matches. I built the Integration Wizard to end that.

It's a reusable, configuration-driven framework that connects Salesforce to ERP and campus systems — PeopleSoft, CMSNext, Formstack, Genius, DocuSign — through MuleSoft and Informatica ETL pipelines, with real-time bidirectional sync, retry-safe error handling, and field-level mapping an admin can change without a deploy.

The result at OneOrigin: 25% higher data accuracy, integrations that used to take sprints stood up in days, and one source of truth across every connected system.
